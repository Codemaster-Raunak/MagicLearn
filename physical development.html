Physical Development

Physical activity is important for children of all ages, but it is especially important for children between the ages of 3 and 8. During this time, children are rapidly developing their motor skills, and physical activity helps to promote healthy growth and development. Physical activity also helps to reduce the risk of obesity, heart disease, and other chronic health conditions.Parents can encourage their children to be physically active by setting a good example. Parents should make sure that they are getting enough exercise themselves, and they should encourage their children to be active by playing games, going for walks, and participating in sports.
